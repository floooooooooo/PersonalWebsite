Cookies

The internet pages partly use so-called cookies. Cookies serve to make our offer more user-friendly, effective and secure. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer and stored by your browser. Different information can be stored within the cookies. Cookies do not harm your computer and do not contain viruses.

Most of the cookies we use are so-called "session cookies". They are automatically deleted after your visit. Other cookies remain stored on your device until you delete them. These cookies allow us to recognize your browser the next time you visit.

The setting of cookies through your web browser can be prevented. However, some functions and offers of our site could be restricted. If you want to deactivate the cookies, you can disable the corresponding option in your browser settings. Saved cookies can be deleted at any time in the settings of the browser.
